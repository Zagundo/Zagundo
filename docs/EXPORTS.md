### Exports & Interop

- **Darwin Core**: CSV/JSON‑LD with `eventDate`, `decimalLatitude`, `decimalLongitude`, `scientificName`, `basisOfRecord`, and trait extensions.
- **iNaturalist**: OAuth, create observation with photos; map traits to observation fields or tags.
- **Backups**: Encrypted iCloud Drive; optional local ZIP export of observations + media + metadata.
